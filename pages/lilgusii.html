Bio

Lil Gusii or Gusii, which real name is Antonio Cintora, was born in Zaragoza, Spain, the 3rd of November of 1999.
He was grown alone with her mother, as his father was never his father, so that made him grow with a depression since the age of 15.
The school never helped tho, as the schoolmates and the bullying were always there.
He started making beats in 2019, when he was going through some rough times with one of her girlfriends, as a way to escape from reality and find
a sound that he enjoyed, but those beats where never used until he started making his own music by the name of br.OK.en.
Some of their songs got really popular on Soundcloud and Spotify, but he realized that all he was doing was trying to copy his idol Lil Peep.
That's where he changed his name to become Gusii, Lil Gusii.
He started doing emo rap/trap songs, also inspired by peep, but started finding his own sound by using different kind of beats from different artist, and getting more
inspiration from his childhood idols: blink-182, My Chemical Romance, and many others.
Now a days, he stills looking for a good sound, and maybe one day he will find it.
In the mean time, he has some songs that you can listen, as he already released 2 albums:

  1. Unknown Album
  2. A Sad Album for Sad Listeners
Also, he has released many singles so far and got so many fans on Instagram and Spotify.
